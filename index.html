With increasing population and demand for food crop yield, the challenges faced by farmers are also increasing. The situation sometimes escalates to the point where the farmers commit suicide. The adjacent graph shows the number of farmers who commited suicide from 2013-2022.

Taking advantage of such situation, some dealer offer them an unfair deal which the poor farmer has to accept as he has no one else to approach.

AgroTech provides a platform for farmers and dealers(middlemen) to approach eachother and make mutually profitable deals.

The algorithm suggests the user a suitable match with whom he can discuss the deal. By analyzing the profit margin and worldwide crop price, the website suggests dealers to farmers and vice-versa.
